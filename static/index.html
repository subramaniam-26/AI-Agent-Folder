About SoilSense AI

The Challenge

Proper agriculture begins with understanding soil nutrition. Lab tests are slow and expensive, making it hard for smallholders to calculate crop selections and fertilizer balances dynamically.

Our Solution

SoilSense AI utilizes a deterministic RGB/HSV visual model to approximate soil types and estimate 12 macronutrients and micronutrients instantly from smartphone pictures.

The Technology Stack

This project is designed as an agentic platform using Google ADK to direct specialist sub-agents, combined with Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers for crop and fertilizer references.
